DOG on Bitcoin

What is DOG? Bitcoin's Rune #3.

DOG•GO•TO•THE•MOON is a community token on Bitcoin. It is not an ERC-20, not a Solana memecoin, and not a company token. It is Rune 840000:3, created with the Runes protocol and tracked on Bitcoin.

View DOG on chain Read the Rune guide
Quick answer

DOG is the largest community token native to Bitcoin.

The simple definition: DOG is a fair-launch Bitcoin Rune with 100 billion supply, CC0 culture, no owner, no team allocation, and a public origin through Runestone holders.

Identity

Rune #3

DOG's Rune ID is 840000:3, making it one of the earliest tokens created with Runes on Bitcoin.

Supply

100B DOG

The total supply is 100,000,000,000 DOG. The thesis treats supply, holders and flows as public data.

Launch

Fair launch

No presale, no team reserve, no discounted insiders. DOG was distributed to Runestone holders.

Culture

CC0

The brand and culture are open. DOG ARMY exists to document, verify and build in public.

Origin

Why Runestone matters.

Runestone was the cultural bridge into DOG: a broad Bitcoin Ordinals airdrop that rewarded active wallets before DOG was distributed.

The origin is public.

DOG was born from the Bitcoin Ordinals and Runes cycle. Runestone holders received DOG, and the DOG Rune can be checked directly through public Bitcoin indexers.
