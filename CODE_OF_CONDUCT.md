# Code of Conduct

DOG Thesis is a public, evidence-first community. To keep it useful and welcoming:

## Our standards

- **Critique claims, not people.** Attack the evidence, not the contributor.
- **Bring sources.** Disagreements are settled with reproducible data, not volume.
- **Stay responsible.** No accusations of crime, identity, or intent beyond what the data proves — this protects every contributor.
- **Be welcoming.** Newcomers, non-coders, and non-native speakers are all valued. Portuguese and English are both fine.
- **No market manipulation.** This is research and education, never price coordination, pump talk, or financial advice.

## Not tolerated

Harassment, doxxing, personal attacks, hate speech, spam, or pressuring others to buy/sell. Defamation — naming names as criminals without proof — hurts the community and the person who posts it.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, label, or remove contributions that break these rules, and may block repeat offenders. To report a problem, open an issue or contact the maintainer ([@dogdamassa](https://github.com/dogdamassa)).

In short: **show the facts, respect the people.**
